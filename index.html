About Me

I am an M.S. student in Department of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing University (NJU) since 2020, supervised by Prof. Tong Lu. I obtained B.S. from Zhejiang University of Science and Technology.

My research interest is computer vision and deep learning. I did some works about semantic segmentation, object detection, and neural style transfer.
