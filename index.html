About Me

I am an MD-PhD candidate in Department of Computer Science and Technology, Nanjing University (NJU) since 2020, supervised by Prof. Tong Lu.

My research interests are computer vision and deep learning. I did some works about semantic segmentation, scene text detection, and neural style transfer.
